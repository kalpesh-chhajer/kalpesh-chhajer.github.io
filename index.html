Our Services

Oral & Oropharyngeal Cancer (Mouth Cancer)
Includes cancer of lips, buccal mucosa and commissure, tongue, floor of mouth, maxillary (upper) & mandibular (lower) jaw, gingivo buccal sulcus (GBS), hard and soft palate, base of tongue, retromolar trigone (RMT), tonsils, lateral pharyngeal wall.
Salivary Gland Disorders
  1. Cancer of parotid, submandibular, sublingual & minor salivary glands.
  2. Stones (sialoliths) of parotid & submandibular glands.
  3. Auto-immune disorders involving glands.
Microvascular Reconstruction
Free fibula flap, Anterolateral thigh free flap, Radial forearm free flap, Medial sural artery free flap (MSAP), Deep circumflex iliac artery free flap (DCIA), Dorsalis pedis free flap, Gracilis free flap, Scapula free flap.
Oral Submucous Fibrosis (OSMF)
  1. Mouth opening surgery for OSMF (for advanced stages)
  2. Intra lesional injections (for early stages)
Precancerous lesions and conditions
Excision of lesions with lasers.
Maxillofacial Surgery
  1. Cyst & tumors of jaw, face, scalp & neck.
  2. Temporomandibular Joint Disorders (TMJ): TMJ ankylosis, internal derangement, myofascial pain dysfunction syndrome (MPDS).
  3. Rehabilitation with implants: maxillofacial implants include zygomatic, pterygoid & dental implants, patient specific implants (PSI).
  4. Sinus surgery: Open and endoscopic assisted (for fungal infections like mucormycosis, aspergillosis)
  5. Orofacial pain: Trigeminal neuralgia, Atypical facial pain, Burning mouth syndrome.
  6. Trauma: Hard and soft tissue injuries.
  7. Orthognathic surgery (Jaw correction surgery).
  8. Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) : diagnosis & treatment.
  9. Facial aesthetic procedures : Botox, fillers, surgical & non-surgical face & neck lifts, rhinoplasty, earlobe correction and blepharoplasty.

Know Your Doctor

Dr Kalpesh Chhajer is a Consultant Oral Onco & Reconstructive Surgeon specialized in management of various benign and malignant pathologies of Head and Neck region. He performs surgeries like Oral cancer resection & reconstruction, management of cyst and tumours of jaws, face and scalp cancers, salivary gland tumours Microvascular free flaps, Salivary gland disorders like sialolithiasis (stone removal), TMJ disorders (Arthoscopy, Arthrocentesis and open joint surgery), Maxillofacial trauma, Jaw correction surgeries (Orthognathic surgery), Maxillofacial Implants to name a few. He has a keen interest in Micro vascular free flaps and 3D printing technology assisted reconstruction of face.

He is trained from premier institute of India like Kidwai Memorial Institute of Oncology, Bangalore, Bapuji educational association and trained in microvascular surgery from Ganga hospital, Coimbatore (Basics) and advanced clinical training from Kolkata. He has also received certified training in Palliative Medicine from Indian Association of Palliative Care. He has also received formal training in TMJ arthroscopy and TM joint replacement by Dr Gary Warburton (USA) and Dr Andrew Sidebottom (UK).

Dr Kalpesh Chhajer is Director of Pushpa's Maxillofacial Cancer & Rehabilitation Centre, Shahibaug, Ahmedabad, Director of Sanjeevani Head and Neck Cancer Surgeons, Ahmedabad and Chief Consultant Oral Onco & Reconstructive Surgeon at GRMI -Rajasthan Hospitals. He has attachments with various hospitals of Ahmedabad like Apollo CBCC cancer centre, Khyati hospital, SAL hospital, BAPS hospital, SBM hospital, Anand Surgical Hospital, Ratan Hospital.

Honorary visiting consultant Oral Onco & Reconstructive Surgeon at BIMS, Bhavanagar and Pushpa Multispeciality Hospital, Balotra, Rajasthan.
